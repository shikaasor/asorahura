# asorahura.com — Solo Expert Repositioning

## What This Is

Complete rebuild of asorahura.com from a confusing dual-brand site (Asor Ahura + Flowmorph) into a high-conversion solo expert platform. The site's singular job: convert CEOs, CTOs, and Operations Managers into paying clients—either through productized services ($5k–$30k+ projects) or paid strategy calls ($50/hr). Flowmorph is retired entirely.

## Core Value

**Scale Your Business Without Scaling Your Payroll** — Asor designs and deploys custom AI automation systems that free up team cognitive bandwidth for work only humans can do, without adding headcount.

## Requirements

### Validated

- ✓ Existing case studies (HR automation, Swiss insurance NLP, document intelligence, healthcare ops)
- ✓ Live demo systems (AI Resume Review, Automation System Demo)
- ✓ Oracle Certified AI Professional credentials
- ✓ Measurable outcomes data (7,200+ hours automated, 39 facilities deployed, etc.)

### Active

- [ ] New unified homepage with pain-first hook and clear CTAs
- [ ] Services page (4 productized tiers: $5k, $5–$15k, $15–$30k, $30k+)
- [ ] Reframed work/case studies page (outcome-first, executive language)
- [ ] AI Readiness Assessment (lead magnet with email capture + PDF report)
- [ ] Redesigned engage form (7 fields max, routing logic to checkout/strategy call)
- [ ] Blog page (LinkedIn/YouTube content hub with CTAs)
- [ ] Checkout page (Stripe per tier, frictionless payment)
- [ ] Email nurture sequence (5 emails post-assessment)
- [ ] Conversion tracking and CRO instrumentation

### Out of Scope

- Flowmorph branding or pages — completely retired
- EU Horizon grant references — removed per direction
- Mobile app — web-first only
- Real-time chat — high complexity, not core to v1
- Video posts — storage/bandwidth costs, defer to v2+

## Context

**Current site problems:**
- Dual identity confusion (Asor + Flowmorph side-by-side)
- Technical messaging ("LangChain, RAG, LLMs") that alienates executive buyers
- No pain-first hook — opens with credentials instead of problems
- Weak Flowmorph page fragments user journey
- High-friction 8-field form with irrelevant questions
- No lead magnet / email capture mechanism
- Case studies buried and misframed (engineers vs operators)
- Blog/articles are dead ends with no CTAs
- No social proof on homepage (buried on engage page)
- No defined conversion funnel

**Target buyer profile:**
- Persona: CEO, CTO, or Operations Manager at scaling business
- Problem: Manual, repetitive operational work eating time and money
- Status: Know they need to automate, don't know where to start or who to trust
- Decision criteria: ROI, integration fit, no vendor lock-in, precision engineering

**Core belief to communicate:**
> "Your business doesn't have a people problem. It has a systems problem. AI can handle the execution. Your team should be focused on the decisions, relationships, and creative work that machines can't replicate yet."

**Tone:** Direct, confident, operator-to-operator. No jargon unless translated to business impact. Specific numbers, short sentences.

## Constraints

- **Brand**: Single brand only (Asor Ahura) — Flowmorph completely retired
- **Positioning**: Executive-first (outcome-focused), not technical
- **Conversion funnel**: Three entry states (cold → assessment, warm → engage, hot → services/checkout)
- **CTA discipline**: Never more than 2 CTAs visible at once; assessment CTA prioritized
- **Assessment scope**: 8 questions, email capture in Step 0, instant PDF delivery
- **Form friction**: Engage form max 7 fields (down from current 8)
- **Pricing**: Transparent tiers with real urgency signals ("Currently booking for [Month]")
- **Email platform**: Must support conditional PDF delivery and segmented nurture campaigns

## Key Decisions
